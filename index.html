Privacy Policy for BestSelf

Effective date: 15th May 2026 Company: BestSelf Contact: abhi.genva@gmail.com

1. Introduction

BestSelf respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BestSelf collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the BestSelf mobile application, website, and related services.

By using BestSelf, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information we may collect

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, username, or profile details.
  • Habit and community activity, such as habits you create, streaks, check-ins, challenges, posts, comments, and group participation.
  • Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system, app version, crash logs, identifiers, and diagnostic data.
  • Usage information, such as interactions with screens, features, and notifications.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support or submitting feedback.

3. How we use information

  • To provide and improve habit tracking, community features, and account functionality.
  • To personalize your experience, including recommendations, reminders, and community suggestions.
  • To maintain app security, detect abuse, troubleshoot issues, and monitor performance.
  • To communicate important service messages, updates, or support responses.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Sharing of information

BestSelf does not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, customer support, or infrastructure partners.

We may also share information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of business assets.

5. Community visibility

Because BestSelf includes community features, some profile details, habits, posts, comments, streaks, or challenge participation may be visible to other users depending on your settings and the specific feature design.

You should only share content in communities that you are comfortable making visible to others within the app.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law.
  • You may manage notification preferences and certain profile visibility settings inside the app, where available.
  • You may contact us to request account deletion or privacy support.

8. Children’s privacy

BestSelf is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law in your region unless specifically stated otherwise. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page. Your continued use of BestSelf after an update means the updated policy will apply.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact BestSelf at abhi.genva@gmail.com.
